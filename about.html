Project 1 Analog Clock

1. Visual Time Awareness

Easier to grasp elapsed time: Analog clocks show the passage of time more clearly. You can visually see how much time is left until the next hour or how long has passed.

2. No Need for Electricity

Many analog clocks run on mechanical movements or long-lasting batteries and don’t require electricity, making them more reliable in power outages.

3. Aesthetic Appeal

Timeless and decorative: Analog clocks often have a more classic or elegant design and can serve as decorative pieces in homes, offices, or public spaces.

Poject 2 Calculator

1. No Power Needed

Operate without batteries or electricity, making them reliable in any environment.

2. Durability

Built to last—many mechanical calculators can work for decades if maintained properly.

3. Better Understanding of Concepts

Devices like the slide rule encourage deeper understanding of mathematical relationships (e.g., logarithms, ratios) rather than just giving quick results.

4. Tactile Feedback

Mechanical keys, sliders, or gears provide physical interaction, which some users find satisfying and easier to use.

5. Educational Value

Great for teaching fundamental math operations, especially estimation, scaling, and significant figures.
